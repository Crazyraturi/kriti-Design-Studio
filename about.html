About Us

Kriti Design Studio was born from a love for heritage fabrics and contemporary silhouettes. We design premium ethnic wear that is feminine, stylish, and expressive.

Mission

To craft personalized ethnic fashion that celebrates every woman’s individuality.

Vision

To be India’s most trusted boutique for luxurious custom couture.

Designer Introduction

Kriti Sharma, our founder and lead designer, blends traditional embroidery with modern tailoring for elegant signature looks.
